### I. Auto click 
* **How to start:** Click the **START** button at the bottom of the app or use the hotkey. The default keybind is `F6` (you can change this in the Hotkey Settings).

* **How to stop:** Click the **STOP** button at the bottom of the app or use the hotkey. The default keybind is `F7`.
### II. Marco
You must record a new macro or load an existing one before you can play it.

* **To record:** Click the **Record/Stop Record** button or use the hotkey (default: `F9`). When you are done recording, press the same button or hotkey again. You can click **Save** to export your macro script as a `.json` file. To reuse a macro later, click **Load** and select the corresponding `.json` file.

* **To play:** After recording or loading a script, click the **Play** button or use the hotkey (default: `F10`).

### III Options
* **Always on top:** Keeps the application window above all other open windows, allowing you to easily monitor the status and remaining time/clicks.
* **Apply limits to Macro:** Ensures that macros follow the time and maximum loop limits set in the "Stop After" section. If this is unchecked, the macro will only play through once and then stop.
* **Show on-screen text overlay:** Displays a floating text indicator on your screen while recording, playing a macro, or auto-clicking. Uncheck this if you want to hide these visual indicators.
### IV. Others
1. **Delay per click:** Determines the time interval between each click. This setting only affects the standard Auto Click function.
2. **Stop after:** Allows you to set a timer or a specific maximum number of clicks/loops. The auto clicker or macro will automatically stop once this limit is reached.
3. **Target position:** Choose where the clicks happen. You can click at your current mouse location or set a fixed X/Y coordinate. To quickly capture a fixed position on your screen, hover your mouse over the desired spot and use the hotkey (default: `F8`).
4. **Mouse Button:** Select which mouse button the app should use for clicking (Left, Right, or Middle).
